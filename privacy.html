Privacy Policy — Arrow Blast

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Arrow Blast ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

The App does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, or contact details. We collect only the following:

  • Game progress & settings — your level progress, stars, coins, hearts, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising & device identifiers — to show ads, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) and limited device/usage information. On iOS we ask for your permission first via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt; if you decline, ads are shown in a non-personalized way.
  • Diagnostics & usage — we may collect anonymous crash and usage data to keep the game stable (via Google Firebase).
  • Purchases — in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment-card details; Apple confirms only that a purchase succeeded so we can grant the item.

How we use information

  • To run the game and save your progress on your device.
  • To display ads (including rewarded videos you choose to watch) and to support the free version of the game.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve performance.
  • To deliver in-app purchases you make.

Third-party services

The App uses the following third parties, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase (diagnostics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Apple (in-app purchases) — apple.com/legal/privacy

Your choices

  • Ad tracking — you can allow or deny tracking when prompted, and change it any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking.
  • Local data — deleting the App removes all locally stored game data from your device.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will remove it.

Data retention

Game progress and settings remain on your device until you delete the App. Anonymous diagnostic data is retained by our providers per their own policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
