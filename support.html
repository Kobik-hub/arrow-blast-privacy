Common questions

I reinstalled the app — are my coins and progress gone?
On iOS, your progress, stars, and coins are backed up to your own iCloud and should restore automatically when you reinstall (make sure iCloud Drive is on in Settings). If something's missing, email us.

I bought "Remove Ads" but ads came back.
Open the shop and tap Restore Purchases — that re-applies any purchases tied to your Apple ID.

A level seems impossible.
Every level is solvable. Try the in-game Hint, or email us the level number and we'll help.
